About

Engineering, patents, investing, and publishing.

John Konnayil Vincent brings together engineering, intellectual property, and investing. He earned a BTech in Kerala and an MS in Information Systems from the University of Hawaiʻi, then spent roughly two decades in Bay Area software. His work led to four U.S. patents in database and query tooling. He later shifted into wealth management and publishes independent research on Seeking Alpha and Substack. Today he is the author of three books on hedge funds and digital assets, and he builds DIY investor dashboards for stock research, screening, watchlists, and macro context. AWS Cloud Practitioner certified (2023–2026).
